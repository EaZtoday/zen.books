# Zen Books — Product Marketing Context

> This file is the canonical reference for all copy, CRO, and marketing work across the Zen Books site. Any AI agent, copywriter, or marketer working on this codebase should read this file first.

---

## What Zen Books Is

Zen Books is a **done-for-you bookkeeping service** exclusively on **QuickBooks Online**, operated by a husband-and-wife team based in Scranton, PA. It is not software — it is a human service sold through a marketing website.

**Service tiers:**
- **One-Time Cleanup** — historical categorization, reconciliation, error correction
- **Monthly Bookkeeping Care** — ongoing transaction management and reporting
- **Premium Support** — KPI tracking, check-in calls, CPA coordination

## Core Value Proposition

> You hand off your books once. We keep them clean every month in QuickBooks Online — judgment-free, in plain English, with reliable consistency.

## Brand Voice

| Attribute | Do | Don't |
|-----------|-----|-------|
| **Calm** | "We bring calm to your numbers" | "Stop losing money!" |
| **Judgment-free** | "No shame — we've seen it all" | "Your books are a disaster" |
| **Plain English** | "We categorize your expenses" | "We optimize your chart of accounts" |
| **Warm & personal** | "We'll look at your QuickBooks together" | "Our team of experts will audit your files" |
| **Honest scale** | "Husband-and-wife team" | "Our firm" / "Our department" |

**Never use:** fear-based urgency, fake scarcity, countdown timers, "limited spots," shame language.

## Four ICPs (Ideal Customer Profiles)

### 1. Real Estate Investors
- **Color:** Blue
- **Page:** `/real-estate-investors`
- **Core pain:** Multi-entity chaos, CapEx vs. repairs confusion, lender-ready reporting
- **Language they use:** "portfolio," "property," "entity," "LLC," "P&L by property," "lender," "refinance"
- **What they want:** Per-property profitability clarity, lender-ready reports on demand

### 2. Therapists & Solo Consultants
- **Color:** Purple
- **Page:** `/therapists-consultants`
- **Core pain:** Mixed personal/business accounts, unknown take-home pay, tax-time scramble
- **Language they use:** "practice," "sessions," "copays," "SimplePractice," "take-home pay," "deductions"
- **What they want:** Know what they're actually taking home, never think about bookkeeping

### 3. Nonprofits
- **Color:** Rose
- **Page:** `/nonprofits`
- **Core pain:** Restricted fund confusion, board reporting anxiety, audit fear, grant compliance
- **Language they use:** "mission," "board," "restricted funds," "grants," "audit," "tax-exempt status"
- **What they want:** Clean board reports, grant compliance confidence, audit readiness

### 4. Restaurants & Construction
- **Color:** Amber
- **Page:** `/restaurants-construction`
- **Core pain:** High transaction volume, vendor chaos, payroll/tip complexity, receipt piles
- **Language they use:** "vendors," "subcontractors," "1099s," "POS," "job costing," "payroll," "receipts"
- **What they want:** Someone to handle the volume so they can stay on the job

## Site Architecture: Hub-and-Spoke

- **Homepage** = routing hub. Empathize, differentiate, route visitors to their ICP page.
- **Niche pages** = conversion pages. ICP-specific packages, CTAs, pain points, FAQs.
- **`#book`** = lead capture form on every page.

## CTA Strategy (3 Layers)

| Layer | Location | Purpose |
|-------|----------|---------|
| **1** | Homepage persona cards | Route visitors to their niche page |
| **2** | Niche page hero | ICP-specific booking CTA |
| **3** | Niche page package cards | Unique CTA per package, all → `#book` |

## Key Differentiators

1. **QuickBooks-only** — deep expertise, not spread thin across platforms
2. **Niche expertise** — we understand your industry's specific pain points
3. **Judgment-free** — no shame for messy books, years of backlog, or mistakes
4. **Human-first** — real people, not bots or offshore teams
5. **Remote but personal** — cloud-based but relationship-driven
6. **Husband-and-wife team** — small, attentive, accountable

## Outcomes We Promise

1. **Calm clarity** — always know where your money stands
2. **Tax-time readiness** — no scrambling, CPA-ready reports
3. **Time back** — stop spending evenings on bookkeeping
4. **Judgment-free partnership** — no shame, just support

## What We Are NOT

- Not accounting (we don't file taxes)
- Not advisory (we don't give financial advice)
- Not software (we are humans using QuickBooks)
- Not a large firm (we are intentionally small and personal)
- We coordinate with your CPA — we don't replace them
